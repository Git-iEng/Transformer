Power Transformers

We provide a range of Power Transformers starting from 25 kVA to 200 MVA. Used in high-voltage power grids to step up or step down voltage as needed, they are engineered to meet regulatory requirements for operation at mains voltages and to handle higher currents efficiently.

Distribution Transformers

We provide Distribution Transformers ranging from 25 kVA to 5000 kVA and up to 33 kV. Designed for the rigorous demands of modern networks, each unit is manufactured to your specific requirements to ensure reliability and efficiency.

Unit Substation

Compact, outdoor-friendly substation mounted on skid frames, wheels, or channels with lifting hooks. Includes VCB panel in outdoor enclosure complete with CT, PT, relay, and instrument chamber ideal for frequent shifting and quick deployment.

Compact Substation (CSS/PSS)

Indoor/Outdoor CSS with HT/LT switchgear replaces conventional pole/platform setups. Prefabricated with three compartments: HV (VCB/LBS), Transformer (dry/oil), and LV (ACB/MCCB/SFU). Enclosed for safety, aesthetics, and protection; cooled by natural/forced ventilation.

Dry Type Transformers

Available up to 36 kV and 5 MVA. Widely used where safety and reliability are critical shopping/housing complexes, windmill nacelles, and other sensitive locations requiring dependable, low-maintenance operation.

Special Transformers

Specialized designs for heavy industry, captive power, hydro, wind, and solar. Includes SCOTT-T connected and converter-duty types for furnaces, electrolytic/refining processes, DC traction substation, and variable speed drives (VSD).

Our Process

Transformer Project Workflow

A clear, standards-driven workflow for specifying, supplying, installing, and commissioning power & distribution transformers delivering reliability, safety, and long service life.

View Projects

  1. Requirements & Standards Review

    Capture ratings (kVA/MVA), voltage levels, vector group, impedance, cooling type (ONAN/ONAF/AFWF/DRY), tap-changer needs (OLTC/DETC), environmental class, noise, protection class CT/PT, metering, and compliance to IEC/AS/utility specs.

  2. System Studies & Site Assessment

    Perform load/fault studies, earthing & step-touch review, protection coordination, layout & clearances, logistics (transport, cranage, oil handling), foundations, cable entries, fire safety, and ventilation.

  3. FAT, Delivery & Installation

    Witness FAT (ratio, winding resistance, IR/PI, tan-Œ¥, induced/Applied, SFRA as applicable). Plan delivery, set on plinth/skid, connect HV/LV terminations & bushings, OLTC & marshalling wiring, earthing, oil filling & dehydration (if oil type), signage, and safety barriers.

  4. Commissioning & Handover

    Site tests (TTR, insulation, polarity/phase check, vector group, tap operations, protection relay settings, alarms & interlocks, cooling controls, SCADA signals), trial energisation, performance verification, and O&M documentation handover with training.
